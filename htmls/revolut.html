    About the Company

    Revolut is a financial services company that specializes in mobile banking, card payments, money remittance, and foreign exchange. Revolut started as a platform for providing prepaid debit cards and currency exchange but has since expanded its services to include cryptocurrency trading, stock trading, insurance, and various other financial

    products. It operates in numerous countries worldwide and is known for its mobile app, which allows users to manage their finances, make payments, and track spending.
